# MEDICORE-GRC-PROJECT
# MediCore Africa GRC Portfolio

## Overview
This repository documents a complete, end-to-end GRC 
(Governance, Risk, and Compliance) program built for 
MediCore Africa Limited — a Nigerian health 
technology company providing Electronic Health Records, 
telemedicine, and health analytics solutions to 400+ 
hospitals and clinics across Nigeria and Ghana.

The portfolio shows practical GRC skills across 
policy development, risk management, compliance assessment, 
and security governance all which are applied to a realistic healthcare 
context governed by the NDPA 2023, National Health Act 2014, 
HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, and NIST SP 800-53.

---

## Company Context
- **Industry:** Health Technology (EHR, Telemedicine, Analytics)
- **Headquarters:** Lagos, Nigeria
- **Employees:** 280 (200 full-time, 80 contractors)
- **Cloud Platform:** Microsoft Azure
- **Key Regulations:** NDPA 2023, HIPAA, GDPR, National Health Act
- **Frameworks:** ISO 27001:2022, NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5, ISO 27799
